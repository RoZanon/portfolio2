About me

Spanish, born in Madrid, working as a freelance in brand identity, graphic design and before long with UX/UI design as well. I have more than 4 years of international experience working in branding, visual merchandising, digital and marketing manager in wine & spirits and cosmetic universe inside the LVMH group - working for brands such as Moët & Chandon, Dom Pérignon, Mercier and Guerlain. In addition, I have worked for YellowKorner as Global Merchandiser and had the experience of co-founding a Sunglasses brand named Barai.

Currently pursuing a Master degree in UX/UI design at K-School Madrid, I hold a Master degree in Fashion and Luxury Brand Management from ESMOD International, in Paris, and a bachelor at the University of Birmingham (UK) in Marketing Management with a double degree in Marketing Management and Advertising at the Universidad Europea de Madrid.

I am confident I have a number of personal qualities that make me successful in the work environment. I work well within a team; I am creative and always thinking outside the box; I am open minded and unhesitating in my ability to cross real and imaginary barriers or limits. Furthermore, I believe in my capacity to solve big challenges.
